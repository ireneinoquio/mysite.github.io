About me

Irene Inoquio is an assistant professor at the Institute of Physical and Mathematical Sciences (ICFM) at the Austral University of Chile, where she has developed most of her professional career. She has conducted research stays at various universities and mathematics institutes, including the University of Rochester in New York, United States; the State University of Campinas in São Paulo, Brazil; IMPA in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil; and IMPAN in Poland, among others.

Her research focuses on dynamical systems, with particular emphasis on ergodic theory, thermodynamic formalism, and ergodic optimization for systems with non-uniformly hyperbolic behavior. Her areas of interest include real and complex one-dimensional dynamics, transcendental entire dynamics, and statistical properties of dynamical systems.
